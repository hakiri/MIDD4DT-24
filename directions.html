Directions

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University is a world-leading university with world-class research in multiple disciplines, making bold advances to break new ground. There are beyond a total of 27,200 students enrolled in taught postgraduate programmes and MPhil/PhD degree programmes.
These programmes lead to the awards of Doctor/Master of Philosophy, Professional Doctorate, Master degree and Postgraduate Diploma, all of which include varying combinations of research and coursework.
